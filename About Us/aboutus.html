Let's Celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi Together

Welcome to the Azad Ekta Mitra Mandal (Trust) Sarvajanik Ganeshotsav Mandal! Established in 1990, we have proudly celebrated Ganesh Chaturthi with unparalleled enthusiasm and joy for over 30 years. As the biggest Cultural Event in Pune City, our festival is a grand and joyous occasion that brings together the community in celebration.

At Azad Ekta Mitra Mandal, our dedicated team members give their 100% to make each Ganesh Chaturthi festival not only memorable but truly extraordinary. Our commitment is to preserving the spirit of Ganesh Chaturthi is unwavering, and we invite you to join us in this vibrant celebration.
